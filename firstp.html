Who are we?

We, The Coconut Library Group are a Literary Group based in Beachtown, The Nether. We collect literary works that inspired generations and might inspire a new one

Why should I join?

Us here at The Coconut Library Group believe that all readers should help each other, and why not a better way to help us with archiving great works of different eras?

We Also offer benefits that would be helpful for any avid Literary Historian, Such as:

- Free Access to our Extended Library that are full of Thought-Provoking Poems and Songs that Influence our everyday culture.

- Great Access to top Unversities and Colleges, as well as excellent Language courses for People Intrested.

- A Starting Fund of $1000 to explore the world and Try to find Literary Art that could change the world.

- A Luxorious Retirement with access to Top Class Tools for Exploration and Self-Reflection

I'm Intrested and want to Join!

Great! to join, we'll need you to meet certain requirements:

- You must be able to speak and read English Properly

- You must understand a wide range of cultures

- You must have a willingness to read and learn with experience

- You must own a mobile device, and a personal computer or laptop

If you fit all those requirements, Please Press the Contact Button and Contact us to Apply There
